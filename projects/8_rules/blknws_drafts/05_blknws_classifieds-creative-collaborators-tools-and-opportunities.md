# Classifieds: Creative Collaborators, Tools, and Opportunities  
*Curated by the Classified Editor*  

Seeking a spark for your next project? Explore our curated classifieds—connecting artists, technologists, and dreamers in search of new worlds.

---

### COLLABORATORS WANTED

**Hybrid Storyworlds Collective**  
Writers, visual artists, and code poets sought for a decentralized project weaving narrative across film, immersive media, and networked experiences. Experimentation with new forms encouraged. Compensation via collective profit-sharing. Pitch session: July 14, online and in LA.

**Sensory Design Lab: “Tactile Futures”**  
Producers and costume designers needed for a hands-on exploration of wearables in live storytelling. Collaboration with AI-enhanced textiles. No prior coding experience necessary—curiosity required. Apply by June 30.

**AI-Driven Memory Archives**  
Archivists, filmmakers, and data artists: Join a team building tools to curate collective memory through participatory video installations. Looking for those interested in the ethics and poetics of synthetic recall. Remote-friendly.

---

### WORKSHOPS & ASSEMBLIES

**Imaginative Intelligences Assembly: Summer Edition**  
A day-long gathering for creators and technologists. Panels, roundtables, and playful experiments—designing creative systems that challenge, nurture, and surprise. July 27, Bradbury Building, Los Angeles. Limited spots.

**Co-Creation Sprints: “Beyond the Screen”**  
Weekly virtual sessions for teams exploring the edge of moviemaking, gaming, and social experience. Hosted by the Possibility Machines Initiative. Open call for facilitators and participants.

**The New Studio: Micro-Residencies**  
Short-term residencies for artists exploring collaboration with algorithmic partners. Access to bespoke creative tools and mentorship. Rolling applications; hybrid format.

---

### TOOLS & PLATFORMS

**Murmur: Distributed Story Engine**  
Beta testers invited for a platform enabling artists and designers to co-create branching, living narratives with audience participation. Supports both text and visual media. Early access perks.

**Kindling AI**  
A creative collaborator designed for moodboarding, script sketching, and sparking dialogue. Now available for trial to independent filmmakers and collectives. Feedback sessions weekly.

**Assemblage Network**  
Join a growing decentralized infrastructure for creative exchange—share assets, ideas, and labor outside traditional industry channels. Open to all disciplines; onboarding events every Friday.

### OPPORTUNITIES

**Call for Proposals: “Experience Containers”**  
Seeking concepts that rethink what a movie can be—immersive, ephemeral, or networked. Grants available for prototypes developed by interdisciplinary teams.

**Mentorship Circle: Navigating Creative Flux**  
Industry veterans and emerging voices meet monthly to share strategies for thriving amid rapid change. Next session: July 10, RSVP required.
